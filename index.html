Delicious Recipes

Here is a quick list of some of the most amazing recipes I have ever eaten in my life. Some of these have been passed down from generation to generation while some have been learned during long nights cooking for my wife and 2 lovely boys.

Lasagna

Want a great meal to feed a huge, tireless family? LASAGNA! I promise you will not be disappointed! hearty and full of meat and sauce, this meal will satisfy the largest of men and the crankiest of kids! This recipe was learned once during a a busy night. I had only 20 mins to prepare a meal and thankfully the oven did the rest!

Ziti

This is a gem! just like the Lasagna, this cooks itself! with a little prep this meal can feed either 1 or an entire family. this recipe was acquired when i was stationed away from my family, my love for pasta does not leave even when i am away! with this recipe i was able to tame the beast that is my hunger all while having enough time to study!

Alfredo

Alfredo Alfredo! Where do I start!?! This dish is my all time favorite! I've fallen in love with so many different variations over the years of my life yet a simple Fettucine Alfredo has just always hit the spot just right. from olive garden to a spicy cajun, you can make this meal your very own!
